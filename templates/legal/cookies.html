This Cookie Policy explains how MechaTechnologies (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies in connection with the Fiscal Integration System (“Service”).

1. Cookies We Use

We use only essential cookies necessary for the operation and security of the Service. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, marketing cookies, or third-party tracking.

2. Essential Cookies

  • Session authentication — Cookies used to keep you logged in and to maintain your session. These are required for the Service to function.
  • CSRF protection — A token cookie used to protect against cross-site request forgery and to secure forms. This is required for security.

These cookies are strictly necessary for the Service and cannot be disabled while using the Service.

3. What We Do Not Use

We do not use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Tracking cookies
  • Marketing cookies
  • Third-party tracking cookies
  • Analytics cookies for behavioral profiling
Compliance statement. The Company does not sell data, access financial records for commercial use, use invoice data for analytics, or share accounting data with unrelated third parties. Cookie use is limited strictly to essential session and security purposes.

4. Duration

Session cookies are removed when you close your browser. CSRF and session cookies used for authentication are set for the duration of your session or as required for security.

5. Contact

For questions regarding this Cookie Policy, contact MechaTechnologies at support@mecha.co.zw or https://mecha.co.zw.
